A New Light for Our Community

Mabuhay! Welcome to this edition of our newsletter, now bearing a new name BANAAG. Meaning ‘glimmer’ or ‘first light of dawn,’ it aims to shine a light on the life, mission, charism, and stories of our Assumptionist community. Our objective remains the same—to connect, to inform, and to inspire by sharing the work being done in our communities and beyond, reflecting our shared journey of faith and service.
